Location

The celebration will take place at Misty River Ranch.

Address: 5690 N Fork Road, Deming, WA 98244

It's about 1 hour 45 minutes drive from Seattle, and 1 hour 30 minutes from Vancouver without traffic.

Friends coming from Canada, please consider reserving extra travel time for border-crossing, and refer to the U.S. and Canada customs and border website for items prohibited from crossing the border.

Lodging: we recommend AirBnB in Deming, or hotels in Bellingham, WA which is 20-30 minutes away from the event location.

FAQ

Can I bring a plus one?

We’re excited to celebrate with you! To keep our celebration intimate, we’re only able to accommodate the guests named on the invitation. We hope you understand and look forward to celebrating with you!

Can I bring my children?

We love your little ones, but due to the nature of the event, we’re hosting an adults-only celebration. We hope you can join us and celebrate this special day!

What should I do if I can't attend the wedding?

We will miss you! Please let us know as soon as possible by updating your RSVP.

What if I have special dietary restrictions?

We want everyone to enjoy the food, so please let us know your dietary preferences or restrictions when you RSVP, and we’ll make sure to accommodate you.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, there are plenty of free parking on-site.

Can I attend just the ceremony or just the reception?

We would love for you to join us for the entire day! However, if you can only attend one part of the day, please let us know in advance and we'll make sure to accommodate you.

Other Questions?

Please don't hesitate to reach out to us for clarification.

Our Story

We met on Hinge—yes, that dating app where you can judge someone based on their profile pictures and their ability to craft a witty bio. And what started as a casual conversation quickly turned into an hour-long discussion about our mutual love for Princess Mononoke.

Since then, from clamming on the shores of Oregon to dancing in New York at a Fujii Kaze concert, every trip has added another cherished chapter. We've crossed oceans to explore the beauty of Japan and traveled back in time by visiting each other's childhood home in China.

Through every journey and every moment, we've discovered that love—like travel—isn't just about the destination. It's about the stories you collect and the partner you share them with. We can't wait to continue this adventure together, and we're so grateful to have you be part of our next chapter.
